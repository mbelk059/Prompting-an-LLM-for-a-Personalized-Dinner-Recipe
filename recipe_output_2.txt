Recipe Name: Grilled Chicken with Roasted Vegetables

Ingredients:
• 1 1/2 pounds boneless, skinless chicken breast
• 2 cups mixed vegetables (bell peppers, zucchini, onions, cherry tomatoes)
• 2 tbsp olive oil
• 1 tsp salt
• 1 tsp black pepper
• 1 tsp garlic powder
• 1 tsp paprika

Instructions:
Step 1 – Preheat the grill to medium-high heat.
Step 2 – Season the chicken breast with salt, black pepper, garlic powder, and paprika.
Step 3 – Grill the chicken for 5-6 minutes per side, or until it reaches an internal temperature of 165°F.
Step 4 – Toss the mixed vegetables with olive oil, salt, and black pepper.
Step 5 – Spread the vegetables on a baking sheet and roast in the oven at 425°F for 20-25 minutes, or until tender.
Step 6 – Serve the grilled chicken with the roasted vegetables.

Chef's Tip: To add extra flavor to the roasted vegetables, try tossing them with a squeeze of fresh lemon juice and a sprinkle of chopped fresh herbs, such as parsley or thyme, before serving.
